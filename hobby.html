Why Drumming?

Drumming has been a passion of mine because it combines physical energy, rhythm, coordination and creativity. It challenges me to improve continually while also being a fun and expressive outlet.

What I’ve Learned

  • Timing and rhythm: Keeping steady beats under pressure.
  • Teamwork: Playing with others improves synchronisation and listening.
  • Discipline: Regular practice has helped in my ROTC training and technical studies.
  • Creativity: Experimenting with different styles and beats keeps things fresh.

Goals

  • Record and produce a drumming track by end of year.
  • Lead a small percussion ensemble in campus or community events.
  • Integrate drumming rhythm concepts into personal productivity and learning routines.
